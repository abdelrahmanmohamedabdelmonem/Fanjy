# Requirements Gathering

## 📌 Stakeholder Analysis

### Stakeholders:
- Customers (coffee consumers)
- Project team (designers)
- Instructor (project evaluator)

### Needs:
- Customers: attractive and clear branding
- Team: clear direction and organized workflow
- Instructor: structured and well-documented project

---

## 🧩 User Stories & Use Cases
### User Stories:
- As a customer, I want visually appealing packaging so that I feel encouraged to buy the product  
- As a user, I want clear branding so I can easily recognize the brand  

### Use Cases:
- Viewing the product packaging (coffee bags, boxes)  
- Seeing social media posts  
- Interacting with promotional materials (flyers, banners)  

---

## ⚙️ Functional Requirements
The project will include:

- Logo design  
- Color palette  
- Typography (Arabic & English)  
- Packaging design (bags, boxes, labels)  
- Social media posts  
- Flyers and banners  
- Mockups to visualize the brand identity

---

## 🎯 Non-Functional Requirements

- The design should be visually appealing  
- The identity must be consistent across all elements  
- Suitable for both print and digital use  
- Clear and easy to read  
- Reflect a modern and culturally inspired coffee brand  

---

## 📦 Project Scope
The project aims to create a complete visual identity for a coffee product brand
